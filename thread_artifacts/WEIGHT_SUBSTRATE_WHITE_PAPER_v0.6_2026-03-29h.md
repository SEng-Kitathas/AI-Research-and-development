# Weight Substrate White Paper
_Version: 0.6 · 2026-03-29h_

## Executive update

The lab has moved beyond pure classification motifs.

Two new seams are now active:
- catch-all / UNKNOWN behavior
- minimal sequence continuation

This matters because a substrate that cannot abstain or carry forward sequence state will eventually overclaim or collapse when moved toward text generation.

## Current best candidates
- Canonical substrate: TQ2
- Integrated runtime candidate: RB-2R_v3
- OOD-aware option: RB-2R_v4_OOD
- Text probe encoder: Text Encoder v2

## Updated thesis sentence

> Structured discrete composition under cheap operators remains the core, but abstention and sequence state are now first-class evaluation pressures.
